That's right, I changed the MOTD. Don't need links to the old country's patreon.

This is a fork of the Goonstation codebase, and mostly I consider this to (right now at least) be a chill place to experiment and learn how the game works. Don't necessarily get too attached to the work you're doing this shift, because if the whole place blows up that just sounds like a win to me. (Obviously, don't be a dick, though.)
No idea what you're doing? For now, check out the Goonstation Wiki's Quickstart guide, but be aware that we can't really speak for the content there. It can be a bit goony.
Questions? I'm sure you can ask just about anyone on here for help, though they might know just as little as you do. Try the adminhelp if any admins are on at the moment.
